Connect TradingView

With TradingView you can send signals to Bitexbot with TradingView Alerts, that will be automagically converted by Bitexbot into a trade on your exchange. Alerts can be sent with Webhook but you need to have a paid TradingView account.

Setup Webhook Alert#

Go to TradingView and click on the clock symbol in the upper right corner. You will now see many options you can choose from to generate a signal. It's necessary to select the option "WebHook URL".

After selecting this option, a text field will appear in which you have to paste the Bitexbot WebHook URL https://api.bitexbot.org/webhook/trade. Next paste the message that you have previously generated on the Webhook Message page and hit the "Save" button.
